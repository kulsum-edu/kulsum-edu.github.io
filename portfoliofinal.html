About Me

I'm Kulsum —
a new-generation
creator

I began my creative journey as a curious child making YouTube videos on KellyTube, and now I'm building fully coded websites like TechWap from scratch.

Whether through sketchbooks or screens, research journals or camera lenses — I use every medium to tell compelling stories and craft meaningful experiences.

I work with languages like C++, Python, and AI tools, all while constantly developing my creative muscle through art and innovation.

This portfolio is a reflection of who I am — an evolving artist, a thoughtful technologist, and a visionary in progress.
